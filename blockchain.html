The Blockchain Certification Training aims to provide intelligence on the structure and mechanism of blockchain. The training focuses on the setting up the personal blockchain environment using Ethereum, understand how to deploy the contract from web and console, etc. Through this training, the candidates would get introduced to the concept of Blockchain and the fundamentals of blockchain and bitcoin.

Target audience

  • The individuals having the intelligence of the basics of the programming and willing to make a career in the Blockchain technology should opt for the course

Prerequisites

Experience with the object-oriented language

The knowledge of the fundamentals of networking like: command line & Linux

Experience with JavaScript
